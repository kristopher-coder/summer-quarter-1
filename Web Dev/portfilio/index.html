    About Me

    Hi, my name is Kris, and I’m 14 years old. I live in San Ramon, California, which is a peaceful and friendly community with lots of parks, schools, and fun places to hang out. It's a great place to grow up, and I’ve made some awesome memories here with my family and friends. I’m really passionate about sports, especially basketball and soccer. I’ve been playing both for a while now, and I love the energy, teamwork, and competition that come with them. Whether I’m shooting hoops or running down the field, I always give it my best effort. Sports have helped me stay active, focused, and disciplined, and they’ve taught me a lot about leadership and working with others. I come from a big family—I have four siblings, which definitely keeps life interesting. There's always someone to talk to, laugh with, or compete against, so things at home are never dull. Being part of a large family has helped me become more patient, responsible, and understanding. One fun fact about me is that I have two middle names. It’s a bit unusual, but I think it makes my full name unique, and I’ve always thought that was pretty cool. In my free time, I enjoy hanging out with my friends, watching movies, listening to music, and trying out new hobbies. I’m also always open to learning new things and challenging myself to grow both in school and outside of it.
